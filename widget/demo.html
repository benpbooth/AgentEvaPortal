What is AgentEva?

AgentEva is an AI-powered customer support platform that helps businesses provide instant, accurate support to their customers 24/7. Our embeddable widget integrates seamlessly into your website with just a single line of code.

Quick Integration

Add this script tag to your website's HTML:

<script src="http://127.0.0.1:8000/widget/widget.js"
data-tenant="demo"
data-api-key="dem_live_nUw5urvXzJvOuquM0cOh_NE8z1BzXTvJ_AcV_X-RDBA"></script>

That's it! The chat widget will appear in the bottom-right corner of your page.
